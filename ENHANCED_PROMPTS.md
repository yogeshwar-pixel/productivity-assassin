# Enhanced Prompt Examples

## After Enhancement

### 1st Violation (Firm - Orange)
```
instagram.com has ZERO connection to "Score 85%+ in semester exams".

You identified "Instagram Reels" as your weakness. Here you are.

9-11 PM is when you always fail. Don't prove it right.

NPTEL is waiting. This scrolling isn't.

Make the choice that future-you won't regret.
```

### 3rd Violation (Harsh - Red)
```
Really? instagram.com AGAIN?

Violation #3 today. You're proving yourself right about "I scroll reels until 2 AM".

"Score 85%+ in semester exams" won't happen by itself.

Every minute here is a minute you'll regret.

My parents expects better. You know better.

Get back to LeetCode before you waste another hour.
```

### 6th Violation (Devastating - Dark Red)
```
STOP LYING TO YOURSELF.

This is the 6th time you've failed today.

"I scroll reels until 2 AM" — You knew this would happen. You always do this.

While you waste time on instagram.com, "My placement chances and parents' expectations" is slipping away.

My parents who took loan for college fees is watching you fail. AGAIN.

You promised yourself "Score 85%+ in semester exams". This isn't it.

NPTEL. NOW. No excuses left.
```

## Key Changes

1. **Direct Address**: "You" statements make it personal
2. **Call Out Patterns**: Explicitly references their own admitted failures
3. **Time Pressure**: "Every minute here is a minute you'll regret"
4. **Accountability**: "watching you fail. AGAIN"
5. **Commands**: "NOW. No excuses left."
6. **Truth Bombs**: "STOP LYING TO YOURSELF"
7. **Future Regret**: "choice that future-you won't regret"

## Psychological Elements

- ✅ Uses their own words against them
- ✅ Escalates with violation count
- ✅ References specific weaknesses
- ✅ Creates urgency and regret
- ✅ No escape from accountability
- ✅ Direct, brutal honesty
